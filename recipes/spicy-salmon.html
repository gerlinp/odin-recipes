Spicy Gochujang Glazed Salmon with Rice

Description

A mouthwatering dish that perfectly balances sweet and savory flavors. With just the right amount of heat from the Korean red chili paste, Gochujang, this recipe takes a simple salmon fillet to a whole new level.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoons Gochujang
  • 1-2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cooked white rice as a side dish
  • Green onions or scallions, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat your oven to broil, setting the stage for creating a perfectly glazed salmon.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the Gochujang, crushed garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, and a pinch of salt, creating a rich and flavorful glaze.
  3. Prepare your baking sheet by covering it with tin foil. Lay the salmon fillet on the foil and generously coat all sides with the tantalizing Gochujang glaze.
  4. Carefully place the glazed salmon on the top oven rack, positioning it as close to the coils as possible for that delectable broiled finish.
  5. After a few minutes under the broiler, your salmon will be beautifully caramelized and cooked to perfection. Garnish with fresh green onions or scallions to add a burst of color and extra flavor.
  6. Serve this tantalizing Spicy Gochujang Glazed Salmon alongside fluffy white rice, allowing the two to complement each other in every mouthful. Don't hesitate to drizzle extra sauce over the rice for an extra kick of spicy goodness.
