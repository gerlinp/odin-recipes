Lemon Pepper Wings

Description

Indulge your taste buds with this delectable Spicy Gochujang Glazed Salmon, a mouthwatering dish that perfectly balances sweet and savory flavors. With just the right amount of heat from the Korean red chili paste, Gochujang, this recipe takes a simple salmon fillet to a whole new level.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of All-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 10-12 chicken wings
  • Melted Unsalted Butter
  • Lemon Pepper seasoning
  • Enough Vegetable Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Preheat both oven and deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Pat the chicken wings with a paper towel to make them as dry as possible. Add a pinch of black pepper on the wings.
  3. Combine the all-purpose flour, chili powder, 1 tsp of pepper, paprika, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Lightly coat the chicken wings with the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  4. Fry the coated wings for about 10 - 15 minutes or until they turn golden brown. Remove the wings and place them on a baking sheet.
  5. Combine the melted unsalted butter with lemon pepper seasoning, and using a brush, coat the wings with the mixture evenly on all sides. Immediately put the wings into the preheated oven.
  6. Bake the wings for 5 minutes in the oven, and as soon as they are out, sprinkle a small amount of salt on them while they are still hot.
